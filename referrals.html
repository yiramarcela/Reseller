REFERRAL PROGRAM

The best compliment you can give us is to refer others to our company. We work hard to build lasting relationships with our affiliates and we appreciate the referrals they give us. That’s why we’re excited to introduce our new Referral Program.

As an affiliate of ASF Homes, you’ll receive a $1,000 for your first referral. For the second referral and each one following, you’ll receive a $2,000. There’s no limit on how many referrals you can make! Also, your Referee’s will receive up to $2,500 in FREE UPGRADES just because you referred them. You will receive a Visa Gift Card for $500 when your friend, client or family member completes the Fixed Cost Construction Contract and the required deposit for their new home. After the Contract requirements have been met you will receive a Visa Gift Card for the entire amount remaining.
